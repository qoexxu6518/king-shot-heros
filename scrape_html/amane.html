Amane (previously known as Mikoto) is an Epic hero in Kingshot, introduced with the June 26, 2025 update. She belongs to the Archer class and can be unlocked through Hero Recruitment and the Oasis & Beyond event.

Sources

  • Hero Recruitment
  • Oasis & Beyond Event

Conquest

Hero Attack: 2,157

Hero Defense: 2,220

Hero Health: 13,320

Sakura Blossom

Amane summons a protective enchantment, increasing all Hero and Escorts’ Attack by 3.5% and Attack Speed by 4.5% for 4s.

Upgrade Preview:

Attack Up: 1.5% / 2% / 2.5% / 3% / 3.5%

Attack Speed Up: 2.5% / 3% / 3.5% / 4% / 4.5%

Fire Sigil

Amane lobs a fiery sigil against the enemy, dealing Attack * 210% damage.

Upgrade Preview:

Damage Up: 150% / 165% / 180% / 195% / 210%

Divine Melody

The dance of the umbrella is the dance of the Blade. Every 3 basic attacks increases Amane’s Attack Speed by 5% until the end of the battle.

Upgrade Preview:

Attack Speed Up: 1% / 2% / 3% / 4% / 5%

Expedition

Archer Attack: +140.11%

Archer Defense: +140.11%

Tri-Phalanx

Amane’s trademark formation increases all Squads’ total Attack by 25%.

Upgrade Preview:

Attack Up: 5% / 10% / 15% / 20% / 25%

Exorcism

Amane’s skill at warding off evil increases Infirmary Healing Speed by 50%.

Upgrade Preview:

Healing Speed Up: 10% / 20% / 30% / 40% / 50%
